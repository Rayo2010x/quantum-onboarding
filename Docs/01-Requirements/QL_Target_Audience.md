## 1. Primary Audiences

### A. Bitcoin Curious (Tier 1)
- **Profile:** Aware of Bitcoin, no Lightning Network experience.
- **Pain Points:** Complexity of LN setup, fear of losing funds, don't understand custodial vs. non-custodial.
- **Portal Goal:** Explain L1 vs L2, recommend beginner wallets (Muun, Wallet of Satoshi), CTA to `quantumbtc.dev`.

### B. Lightning Network Users (Tier 2)
- **Profile:** Actively uses LN, understands channels and invoices.
- **Pain Points:** Concerned about LN security long-term, interested in quantum threats.
- **Portal Goal:** QuantumShield tab — in-depth roadmap of ECDSA vulnerability, softfork vs hardfork paths.

### C. Bitcoin Developers & Researchers (Tier 3)
- **Profile:** Protocol-level knowledge, follows BIPs, follows NIST PQC standards.
- **Pain Points:** Lack of consolidated research on Bitcoin's quantum vulnerability & transition paths.
- **Portal Goal:** Deep technical content (QuantumShield), links to open-source research, Nostr/GitHub community.

## 2. Secondary Audiences

- **Media & Journalists:** Need a clear, non-technical summary of the project's mission.
- **Donors & Supporters:** Need to understand impact of contributions → link to `quantumbtc.dev` donation flow.
